AGE OF CROWNS Gold: 0 (+0/turn) Cities: 0/80 (need 48) Turn 1
How to play: tap an army figure, compose your force in the bar, press Choose target, then click any highlighted cell, city, or enemy army — hover previews the route. Armies roam freely across the world (foot 3 cells, cavalry 5, siege 2); enemy armies and cities must be fought. Take a city to capture its whole province. Forced adds +2 cells but troops arrive winded (−20%). Cities recruit; coastal cities build War Galleys, and holding a sea uncontested lets armies cross it. Research in the Tech tree.
